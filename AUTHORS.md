# Authors and Upstream Credits

This project, `yakuza-gmt-gmd-blender`, combines functionality from two upstream Blender addons:

- `yakuza-gmt-blender` by SutandoTsukai181
  - Provides GMT animation import/export and CMT camera animation support.
  - Upstream project: `https://github.com/SutandoTsukai181/yakuza-gmt-blender`

- `yk_gmd_io` by Samuel Stark, also known as TheTurboTurnip
  - Provides GMD model import/export support.
  - Upstream project: `git@github.com:theturboturnip/yk_gmd_io.git`
  - Integrated from commit `32016a1`.

Both original authors should be credited when this combined addon is distributed or referenced.
